Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 02, 2025

Welcome to BongDate. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. If you do not agree with the terms of this policy, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect information that you voluntarily provide during registration and profile setup, such as:

  • Name or nickname
  • Phone number (used for authentication)
  • Gender, birthday, bio, interests
  • Photos and media you upload

Location Information

Approximate location is requested to show you relevant matches near you.

App & Usage Information

We collect interaction data like likes, profile views, and matches to provide core app functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To show nearby and compatible matches.
  • To enable chat and interaction between users.
  • To prevent fraud, ensure safety and legal compliance.
  • To improve app performance and user experience.

3. Sharing of Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share minimal data with:

  • Firebase (for authentication, database, file storage)
  • Google Play Billing (for subscriptions & payments)
  • Law enforcement if legally required

4. Data Security

All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. However, no system is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

5. Age Restriction

BongDate is strictly for users aged 18+. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

6. Account & Data Deletion

You may delete your account & associated data at any time.

How to Request Deletion

  • In the App: Profile > Settings > Account > Delete Account
  • Email us: bongdateapp@gmail.com (Include your registered phone number for verification)

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Phone number and login credentials
  • Profile details (bio, interests, etc.)
  • Uploaded photos/videos
  • Likes, matches, and discovery data
  • Chat history

Retention Policy

For fraud prevention and legal obligations, certain non-identifiable logs may be retained for up to 30 days before being permanently deleted.

Once complete, deletion is irreversible.
